The Process

Live in 14 days,
without the headache.

We handle everything from strategy to setup. You get a working AI system — not a DIY tool to figure out yourself.

1

Day 1 — Discovery Call

We learn your business in 30 minutes

We ask you about the calls you get, the questions that come up most, how you book appointments, and which tools you're already using. That's all we need. You don't have to prepare anything — we guide the whole conversation.

2

Days 2–12 — We Build It

Custom AI built, tested, and ready

Our team configures your AI, trains it on your services and FAQs, connects it to your phone number, and integrates your booking system. You'll get a preview to review before anything goes live — and we iterate until it sounds exactly right.

Your AI starts working. You don't lift a finger.

Your AI goes live on all your channels. From day one it's handling calls, capturing leads, and booking appointments. We monitor performance and send you a weekly report — calls handled, leads captured, revenue attributed.
