FAQ

Common questions

How long does setup take?

14 days from your first discovery call to going live. We handle everything — you review and approve before anything is switched on.

Is there a contract?

Yes, 36-month service agreement. This allows us to invest in building and optimising a system that's specifically tuned to your business. The longer horizon means your AI gets significantly smarter over time.

What if I get more calls than my plan?

Overage is billed at $0.60/min beyond your included minutes. Most clients stay within their plan — we help you select the right tier during the discovery call.

Can I upgrade my package or tier later?

Yes, anytime. You can move up a tier (Lite → Standard → Pro) or add more channels (Package A → B → C) as your business grows.

Do you handle all the technical setup?

Completely. You don't touch any configuration. We provision your phone number, integrate your booking system, and train the AI — then hand you a working system.

Can I change what my AI says after it's live?

Technical maintenance, uptime, and hosting are always included in your monthly fee — that never changes. Once your system is trained, tested, and live, revisiting and reconfiguring its trained answers, scripts, pricing, or FAQs is a separate SGD $300 per request, since it means our team going back into your live configuration. We recommend reviewing everything carefully before go-live to get it right the first time.

What happens after the 36-month term?

Your system continues running and you move to a month-to-month arrangement. We'll also discuss what improvements are available with any newer AI capabilities.

What's the difference between Package A, B, and C?

Package A — Frontline Reception is the AI voice receptionist only (phone calls), from SGD $145/month. Package B — Conversion System adds an AI website chatbot that books appointments from chat, from SGD $600/month. Package C — OmniChannel Front Desk adds WhatsApp, Instagram and Facebook on top of that, from SGD $899/month. Each package has Lite, Standard and Pro tiers priced by monthly call volume.

Which package and tier should I choose?

Tier is driven by phone call volume: under 120 calls/month fits Lite, 120–250 calls/month fits Standard, and 250+ calls/month fits Pro. Package is driven by which channels you need covered — phone only (A), phone plus website chat (B), or phone, website, WhatsApp, Instagram and Facebook (C). We help confirm the right combination during the discovery call.
